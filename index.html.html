About Us

Our Mission

Champions' Ambassadors is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting sports clubs, particularly at the grassroots level. We understand the challenges faced by community sports organizations and provide the necessary support to help them succeed.

Founded in 2015, we've helped over 200 sports clubs streamline their operations, improve their administration, and focus on what really matters - developing athletes and building strong communities through sports.

Our team consists of former athletes, sports administrators, and passionate volunteers who believe in the transformative power of sports.

Our Services

Our Services

Sports Administration

We handle club registrations, league applications, and all necessary paperwork to ensure your club operates smoothly within official frameworks.

Documentation Assistance

From player registration to insurance paperwork, we help with all documentation needs to keep your club compliant and protected.

Fundraising Support

We provide guidance and resources to help your club secure funding through grants, sponsorships, and community fundraising initiatives.

Volunteer Training

We offer training programs for club volunteers in administration, coaching basics, and child protection to build your club's capacity.

Club Development

Strategic planning and organizational development services to help your club grow sustainably and achieve its long-term goals.

Networking Opportunities

We connect your club with other organizations, potential sponsors, and resources in the sports community.
